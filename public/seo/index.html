What is SEO

Search Engine Optimisation, better know as SEO is the process of increasing traffic to your website by ranking high in SERPs (search engine result pages). If you're a business owner or a marketing consultant and you want to improve the visabilty of your website, it is likely you've heard about SEO. SEO can bring huge benefits to any business, large or small. For small businesses it offers a great opportunity to compete with larger businesses due to SEOs organic nature.
